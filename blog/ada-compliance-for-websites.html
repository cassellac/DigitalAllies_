How to Get Started

  1. Audit your site

    • Tools: WAVE, axe, Lighthouse
    • Manual checks: keyboard-only navigation, screen reader tests

  2. Fix the basics

    • Add alt text
    • Improve contrast
    • Ensure forms and navigation are accessible by keyboard
    • Add captions and transcripts for media

  3. Test with real users

    • Recruit testers with disabilities
    • Use feedback to improve usability

  4. Publish an accessibility statement

    • Outline your commitment
    • Provide a contact for accessibility issues

  5. Maintain accessibility

    • Make audits part of your website update process
    • Train your team in accessibility best practices
